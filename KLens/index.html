Turn your phones into a powerful scanner
Lens works as your high - quality personal portable scanner. Simply snap a photo of any physical documents and digitize within matter of seconds using your mobile device's camera. Klens resolves the common issues like blurry images, odd angles shadows by automatically adjusting them to produce sharp, professional-looking results. Effortlessly trim edges, correct colors, and reduce glare, adding mire contrast and clarity than a regular camera photo could achieve.

Documents missing information or need adjustments
Review and edit the image before you save it to ensure it captures every detail you need. Add captions, annotations, or even digital signatures directly to the image all without needing to print it.

Stay organized
Whether you're using Lens for work, school, or personal organization, it's tailored to help you stay on top of your documents. Organize and digitize forms, receipts, notes, contracts, and more with a tap on camera icon on Klens app. Send your scanned documents via email or messaging apps directly from the app, reducing the chance of losing or misplacing important documents
